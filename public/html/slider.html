HYUNDAI VERNA


The Hyundai Verna is a compact sedan that comes with both petrol and diesel engine options. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed manual gearbox as standard, with an optional 6-speed automatic transmission available for the petrol engine. The Verna also comes with features such as LED headlamps, touchscreen infotainment system, automatic climate control, and multiple airbags for safety. Overall, the Hyundai Verna is a well-rounded compact sedan that offers good performance, comfort, and safety features.
